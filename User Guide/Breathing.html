Description

Hate QUEUING in Hospital just for regular breathing rate checking????????

This new Breathing Rate System provides a solution for both patients and doctors to avoid the WAITS and QUEUES!!! Patients can periodically self-record their breathing rate according to their doctors instructions anywhere as long as they have their cellphone. Besides that, they could also view their own breathing rate history. On another hand, Doctors also have easy access to all patients breathing rates, without actually meeting the patients directly.

This system was designed to be simple to use and friendly even for aged people.

Features

  • Register and Login
  • Patients
    • Record breathing rate using the stopwatch provided
    • View history
  • Doctors
    • View all patients
    • View the data for each patient

Brief User Guide

  • Register
    1. Click on register button on the front page
    2. Fill in the Title, Username, First Name, Last Name, and Password, and click OK
  • Login
    1. Click on Login button
    2. Fill in using the registered username and password
  • For Patients
    1. Once login, patient will be redirected to breathing recording page
    2. Start the stopwatch to start recording
    3. Stop the stopwatch to stop recording
    4. Reset the stopwatch to reset the count
    5. Once the stopwatch reach 60s, patient can enter their breathing count in the text box specified
    6. Click Send once finished
    7. Use menu on the top right of the page to go to the view history page and to go back to the breathing recording page
  • For Doctors
    1. Once login, doctor will be redirected to doctor page that contains list of all patients
    2. Click on any patient to view their breathing history
