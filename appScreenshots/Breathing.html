Breathing Rate System


Description

Hate QUEUING in Hospital just for regular breathing rate checking????????

This new Breathing Rate System provides a solution for both patients and doctors to avoid the WAITS and QUEUES!!! Patients can just periodically self-record their breathing rate according to their doctors instructions at home or anywhere. Besides that, they could also view their own breathing rate history. On another hand, Doctors can also view all their patients breathing rate without actually meeting the patients directly.

This system is designed to be as SIMPLE as possible. It's GUARANTEE to be user friendly and easy to use even for aged people.

Features

  • Register and Login
  • Patients
    • Record breathing rate using the stopwatch provided
    • View history
  • Doctors
    • View all patients
    • View the data for each patient
